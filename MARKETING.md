# Brain Vault - Growth & Sustainable Strategy (v0.4.0)

We pivot from a "Bookmark Tool" to a **Neural Recall Engine** targeting high-intent power users.

---

## 1. High-Trust Pricing Model
We reject low-margin pricing to signal quality and sustainability.
- **Monthly**: $1.99 (The Knowledge Worker)
- **Yearly**: $14.99 (The Professional Researcher)
- **Lifetime**: $49.99 (The Sovereign Mind)

## 2. Competitive Positioning: "Recall vs. Storage"
| Feature | Pocket / Raindrop | Brain Vault |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Messaging** | "Read it later" | **"Never lose an idea again"** |
| **Trust** | Venture Backed / Cloud | **Self-Sovereign / Local-First** |
| **Price Signal** | Budget | **Sustainable / Premium** |
| **Recall** | Search Bar | **Semantic Neural Chat** |

## 3. Target Power Segments
- **Analysts & Researchers**: High-volume ingestion, zero tolerance for cloud AI.
- **Founders & Creators**: Need to synthesize research for content/decisions.
- **Privacy Maximalists**: Need the iron-clad promise of Local-First + E2EE.

---

## 4. Addressing Longevity Anxiety
- **Data Export**: Guarantee users "You can leave at any time" with full JSON exports.
- **Local-First**: Market Brain Vault as a "Local Binary" that keeps working even if we disappear. This is the ultimate trust pillar.

## 5. Visual "Hook"
Show the **Brain Chat** answering a complex question about a saved PDF/Article. That is the moment of value.
